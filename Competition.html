The competition process you've outlined sounds engaging and
supportive of emerging filmmakers. Here's a breakdown of how
the competition works:

Competition Rules

Film Submission:

Filmmakers from around the world submit their films to
Talentwood's competition.

Selection of Top Directors

After the submission period, a panel of judges review the films and
selects the top 10 directors whose work stands out.

Crowd Funding for 30-Minute Films

The 10 selected directors are given opportunity to direct a
30-minutes duration film.

These filmmakers embark on a crowd funding campagin to secure
the necessaryfunds for their projects. This approach empowers
filmmakers to engage with their audience and generate support for
their creative endeavors.

Telecast and User Engagement:

Once the 10 films are produced,they are telecast on Talentwoood's application and
social media platforms.

User engagement plays a cruicial role in determining the winner. Users can
express their appreciation for the films through likes,share and comments.

Winner Announcement:

The film that receives the highest user response, as measured by likes,
shares,and comments, is declared the winner of the competition.

Winner Announcement:

The Winning film receives 40% of the cash prize and rewards, recognizing
the director's creative achievement.

Producers of the winning film are awarded 50% of the cash prize,
acknowledging their support and contribution to the project.

Additionally, one execptional director may be chosen to direct a future
film, offering an exciting opportunity for the career growth.
