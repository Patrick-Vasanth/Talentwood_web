SUBMISSON GUIDELINES

Who can enter the Talentwood Awards?

The Talentwood Awards is open to all independent short films/filmmakers anywhere in the world that meet the following criteria:

  • Short films: Total runtime for shorts may be no more than 40 minutes

What do I need to do to enter the Talentwood Awards?

  1. Complete the entry form and submit your film
  2. Arrange online payment of entry fee
  3. Submit completed entry form and entry fee

1. Complete the entry form

Complete the Online entry form, which includes a link to your film. Only one entry form is required regardless of the number of categories you wish to enter. However, if you wish to enter more than one film, you must complete a different entry form for each title.

2. Arrange online payment of entry fee

The 2023 Final Deadline entry fees are as follows:

  • Short Film categories: 1,000/-

    • 3. Submit completed entry form and entry fee

If there are any additional documents or materials to be submitted, please include them as specified in the entry guidelines

    Submission Guideline

    Originality Matters:

    We celebrate originality and creativity. All submissions must be entirely
    your own work. Plagiarism or the use of copyrighted material without
    permission is not allowed.

    Film Length:

    To keep the competition engaging and accessible, short films should
    not exceed 20 minutes in length. Concise storytelling often leaves a
    lasting impact.

    Genere Freedom:

    We welcome short films of all genres – drama, comedy, animation,
    documentary, and more. Let your imagination run wild and choose the
    genre that best fits your vision.

    Rights and Permissions:

    By submitting your film, you grant us the right to showcase it on our
    platform and during events. Ensure you have all necessary permissions
    for any copyrighted material used in your film.
